I'm a research assistant at the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics and a PhD candidate at the Biozentrum, University of Basel. My main interests involve statistical modelling and development of computational workflows. In my (little) spare time I train martial arts, watch movies or read blogs related to mathematics and data analysis.

I hold a MSc degree in Biotechnology as well as BSc in Biotechnology and BSc in Computational Mathematics from Jagiellonian University (Poland).
